## About me

I'm a QA Engineer and Product Owner with hands-on experience building government digital platforms that serve real citizens. I bridge the gap between technical teams and business goals — writing test plans, leading sprints, mapping requirements, and shipping reliable software.

Currently working at the **Information Technology Agency of the State of Tocantins (ATI)**, where I've contributed to platforms ranging from public health tools to citizen participation portals.

- 🧪 **Test automation** with Selenium, Playwright and Cypress
- 📋 **Requirements engineering** and business analysis
- 🚀 **Product ownership** — backlog, sprints, acceptance criteria
- 🎨 **UI/UX prototyping** with Figma
- 🌎 **English C1** — comfortable in technical and business contexts
